Installation Guide for BurlaOS

Overview

This guide will walk you through the process of installing BurlaOS and creating a user account. Follow these steps carefully to ensure a successful installation.

Pre-Installation Requirements

Before you start, make sure you have the following:

  • Minecraft with ComputerCraft installed.
  • Internet connection to download BurlaOS.
  • Minimum hardware specifications:
    • Operating System: ComputerCraft++
    • Minimum Monitor: Advanced Monitor
    • Minimum Computer: Advanced Computer

Installation Steps

Step 1: Download BurlaOS

Click the buttons below to download BurlaOS. One button downloads the ZIP file, another downloads the LUA installer, and the third copies the Pastebin URL to your clipboard. Make sure to save the downloaded file in an accessible location on your computer..

Download BurlaOS - ZIP Download BurlaOS Installer - LUA Botón para Copiar al Portapapeles

Step 2: Extract the Files

If you downloaded the ZIP version, extract its contents to the root directory of a ComputerCraft computer. If you downloaded the LUA installer, run it from a ComputerCraft computer. The command 'get pastebin' will also download the LUA installer directly from the console of the ComputerCraft computer.

Step 3: Install BurlaOS

Follow the instructions provided in screen promts.

Creating a User Account

Step 1: Launch BurlaOS

After the installation is complete, launch BurlaOS. You will be prompted to create a new user account.

Step 2: Enter User Information

Enter your desired username and password. Ensure that your password is strong and secure.

Step 3: Complete the Account Setup

Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the account setup. Once done, you should be able to log in with your new account.

Troubleshooting and Support

If you encounter any issues during installation or account creation, please contact our support team at support@burlasoft.com.
