PCR Master

PCR Master is specialized software for Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) analysis. Ideal for biochemists, molecular biologists, and lab technicians.

  • Analyzes DNA sequences to determine molecular mass, GC content, and melting temperature.
  • Provides visualization tools for base composition charts.
  • Generates complementary sequences for PCR experiments.
  • Performs advanced calculations for determining dimers and codons.
PCR Master

Dilution Master

Dilution Master is a software tool for dilution calculations in laboratories. Ideal for chemists and technicians needing to simplify complex dilution calculations.

  • Calculates concentrations of diluted solutions.
  • Includes standard dilution tables for various chemicals.
  • Generates detailed dilution reports for documentation.
Dilution Master

ELISA Master

ELISA Master is a comprehensive tool for planning and managing ELISA experiments. Ideal for laboratory technicians, researchers, and scientists conducting ELISA tests.

  • Experiment Planning: Plan and organize ELISA experiments with detailed inputs for samples, dilution ratios, and volumes.
  • Data Export: Export results in CSV format for record-keeping and further analysis.
  • Interactive Charts: Visualize dilution calculations with bar charts for better understanding.
  • Accessibility: Designed with accessibility in mind, with clear labeling and keyboard navigation.
ELISA Master

Ez Normalizer

Ez Normalizer is designed for laboratory professionals to normalize sample results using the z-score method, offering intuitive controls for data management and easy-to-use features.

  • Normalization: Calculates normalized results using the z-score method.
  • Data Management: Add, remove, and clear sample data with intuitive controls.
  • Chart Visualization: Visualize original and normalized results with dynamic bar charts.
  • CSV Export: Export sample results as CSV for easy sharing and record-keeping.
Ez Normalizer

EzANOVA

EzANOVA is a comprehensive tool for Analysis of Variance (ANOVA) studies. Ideal for statisticians, researchers, and analysts who need to evaluate differences among group means and perform advanced statistical analysis.

  • ANOVA Calculations: Perform one-way ANOVA to analyze variance among multiple groups.
  • Visual Charts: Generate bar charts to visually represent group means and other ANOVA results.
  • Export Options: Export results to CSV for further analysis or sharing with colleagues.
  • Detailed Reports: View detailed ANOVA reports with key metrics like F-statistics and p-values.
